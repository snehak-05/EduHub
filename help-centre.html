Frequently Asked Questions

How can I reset my password?

+

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password?". Follow the instructions to reset it.

What should I do if I can't access a course?

+

If you are unable to access a course, please check your enrollment status. If you are enrolled and still have issues, contact support.

How can I contact support?

+

You can contact support via the "Contact Us" form on our website, or email us at Eduhub@gmail.com.

If you need further assistance, please reach out to our contact us.
